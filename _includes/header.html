Relationality is a call to invest in the transformative and structurally undervalued work of enabling human connection. Grounded in foundational scientific principles and movement organizing practice, Relationality explores how we create the conditions for relationship, why this work receives to little investment, and how learning to invest in relational work could radically transform our lives and our world.

Relationality is available now from North Atlantic Books wherever books are sold.
